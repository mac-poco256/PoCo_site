The Window Menu

The window menu work that operate main window and sub windows.


Relative to the Main Window(the Document Window)
Minimize A window of current document on the PoCo.app puts in the Dock.
Minimize All All window of document on the PoCo.app put in the Dock.
Zoom A window of current document on the PoCo.app expand into full-screen or resume from full-screen.
Bring All to Front All window opened by the PoCo.app bring front than other applications.
Relative to Sub-windows
View Window Open the view window.
Palette Window Open the palette window.
Tool-bar Window Open the tool-bar window.
Pen-style and Tile-pattern Window Open the pen-style and tile-pattern window.
Layer Window Open the layer window.
Color Pattern Window Open the color pattern window.
Information Window Open the information window.
