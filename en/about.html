What is the PoCo.app

The PoCo.app is graphic editor for Mac(Mac OS X, OS X, macOS). The PoCo.app especially feature is only support for 8-bits color.

The PoCo.app features following:

  • Support for 8-bits color graphic only.
    In other word, index-color tool that choose 256 colors from 16 million Colors.
  • Specialize in drawing and color mixing with tile pattern(organized dither).
    Pattern density by degrees that can tint of color, shade of color and mix of color.
  • Also, can edit dot picture(pixel art graphhics). However, this is not mainly purpose.
    You may suppose that it is the Dot Picture(Pixel art graphics) when you heard 256 colors picture. However, the PoCo.app can edit more large size than dot picture(pixel art graphhics), as printable image(ex. A4-300dpi(approximate w:3,500 dot h:2,500 dot)).So, mainly purpose of the PoCo.app is so-large dot picture(very large pixel art graphhics).
  • Support for structure of multi-layer. Support for restrict of color.
    When there are above layers, below layers are hidden.
    When drawing restrict color(color attribute set to restrict), such color is not over-write.
  • Support for the Pen tablet.
    Line width and tint of color depend on pressure. Besides, eraser of the Pen also support.
  • Color mixing is able to do.
    Although above mentioned that tile pattern, blend white with black, red with blue, lightness with darkness, and paleness with deepness support.
    Auto choose from 256 colors based on result of mixing.
    Color mixing that support for RGB(Red, Green and Blue). Besides support for HLS(Hue, Lightness, and Saturation).
